who am I

My name is Katie. Before I joined RTS, I was struggling in my acedemics because of the personal things I was going through at home. It was also difficult because my old school had multiple requirements to graduate. I stopped going to school for almost 1 month because I was feeling a little depressed. And then I found the Road to Success Program.

What does rts mean to me?

RTS to me feels like family. It is a place where we can all be united and help each other motivate one another to graduate. Everyone in RTS gets along very well. The staff and students in RTS are very truthful. My classmates are always checking in on me if I need support on my work, but also checking up on me are the students I don't have in class with me. We help support each other in school in general and even outside of school.

why does this project matter to me

This project matters to me because everyone gets along very well, but there should be a more comfortable space in the common area. If we could raise money for furniture, it would help the generations to come feel comfortable with each other. This would also be helpful because the students could focuse more on their work and their overall motivation to show up every day knowing they will be supported.
