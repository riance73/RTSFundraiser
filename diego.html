Who am I?

I am a person who is curious and likes to explore different worldviews and beliefs. For example, I enjoy having conversations with my friends to challenge their perspectives so they can think more deeply and question things they might have only believed because of how they were raised.

What does RTS mean to me?

RTS is a place where I can reach my goal of graduating and learn to discipline myself to do better. In this program, I want to try my best to get back on track and discover the career path I want to pursue.

Why does this project matter?

This project matters because we want to fundraise to improve our community and make our space feel more welcoming for future students. I believe this project is important because it helps students feel more at peace and comfortable without sacrificing our learning environment.
