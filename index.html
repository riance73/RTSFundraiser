RTS (Road To Success)

RTS students spent this semester taking over the design of their environment because they wanted to create a space that actually reflects who RTS is. To change that, the students went beyond just looking at catalogs; they met with professional interior designers, researched how different materials and environments can actually shift your mood, and even learned to code to bring our Project-Based Learning (PBL) ideas to life. They've put in the work to understand the science and tech behind a productive, welcoming space, and now we’re looking to turn those designs into a reality. By supporting our fundraiser, you’re investing in the skills we’ve built and helping us create a legacy of student-led innovation here at Road To Success.
