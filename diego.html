Who am I?

I am a person who is curious and likes to see different worldviews and what people believe in. An example of this could be conversations I have with my friends to test their worldviews so they can think about it harder and question things they might have only believed because they were raised to believe them.

What does RTS mean to me?

RTS is a place where I can reach my goals to graduate and I can discipline myself to do better. In this program, I want to try my best so I can get back on track and soon I will find the career I want to pursue.

Why does this project matter?

This project matters because we want to fundraise money to help better our community and make our space feel more welcoming for upcoming students. I think this project is good to do because it helps students feel more at peace and comfortable without sacrificing our learning.
