Who am i

I am a high school student in RTS. Before I got accepted into RTS I was not performing well in high school. I was always trying to fit in with the wrong friend groups and on top of that the school itself was not a good match for me. In my opinion, the staff had favorite students and the administration kept laying off the black teachers whom I had developed close relationships with. “I lost all my motivation to do well!”.

What does RTS mean to me

What RtS means to me. It is a program that actually believes in second chances. It gives each student an opportunity to finish high school at their own pace. RTS has so many supportive staff and the way they treat you is overall amazing! For an example, Ms. Torres and Mr. Higginbottom will call you every morning and check up on you. Then ask you if you're coming to school. Ms. Torres has the brightest energy which motivates me to push myself to come to school even on those days when I don’t feel like getting out of bed.

Why does this project matter

This project matters because our common space is just a room with a few bits of furniture thrown into it. When new students come into the program, they always stand in the corner behind the computer cart or stare at the clock waiting for the day to end. This can be hard for a person who is shy or does not make friends easily. Another reason for our project is “we only have two small double seated couches. Only 4 people can sit on it. This means many of us have to stand up during free time instead of recharging our bodies for the afternoon activities. Many students actually want to sit down. This project for us is to raise money together as a group and get new furniture for our common space so it can be a better place for everyone.
